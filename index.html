About Us

We like dumplings.

We're a free speech community where you can actually be yourself.
Here you can hang around, send memes, sell your soul to the casinos, plan terrorist attacks, and play games with us.
You can even meet real other human beings that actually exist in the real world just like you do!
You can and should do whatever you want and be free.

Don't know what free feels like?
Try one of the following:
- Share your obsessions and favourite fandoms with us
- Invite us to play some sketchy totally-paid-for-games-from-totally-safe-websites with you
- Send us pictures of your feet (if you're into that)
- Ask us for help with something technical even if it's unethical
- Give us your life story
- Call someone a nigger
- Show us what projects you're working on, even if they're questionable
- Be the edgelord you've always dreamed to be

Still unsure whether there's something for you here?
